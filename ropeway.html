Ropeway

The Udaipur Ropeway is a popular tourist attraction located in the city of Udaipur, Rajasthan, India. The ropeway offers visitors a breathtaking view of the city's stunning landscapes, including the beautiful Lake Pichola, the Aravalli Hills, and the iconic City Palace. The ropeway ride starts from the Deen Dayal Park and takes visitors to the Karni Mata Temple, located atop the Machhala Hill. The temple is dedicated to Karni Mata, a revered saint of Rajasthan, and is a popular pilgrimage site for locals and tourists alike. The ride lasts around 5-6 minutes, covering a distance of approximately 750 meters, and provides visitors with a bird's eye view of the city's beautiful scenery. The ropeway ride is a unique and exhilarating experience, offering a memorable way to explore the beauty of Udaipur. Visitors can also enjoy a variety of food and souvenir stalls near the ropeway station, making it a perfect spot for a fun day out with family and friends.
