Pichola Lake

Pichola Lake is a beautiful artificial lake located in the heart of the city of Udaipur, Rajasthan, India. The lake was created in the 14th century by Maharana Udai Singh II and is renowned for its stunning beauty and historical significance. The lake is surrounded by beautiful hills, palaces, temples, and ghats, making it a popular tourist attraction and a favorite spot for photographers. The lake is home to several islands, including Jag Niwas, which is home to the famous Lake Palace Hotel, and Jag Mandir, which is a popular tourist attraction. The lake is also a popular spot for boating, with several types of boats available for rent. The lake's serene and tranquil surroundings make it a popular spot for romantic evenings and peaceful walks. Pichola Lake is a must-visit for anyone visiting Udaipur, offering a glimpse into the city's rich history and natural beauty.
